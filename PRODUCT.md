# Product

## Register

product

## Users

ai-draw serves illustrators, designers, content creators, and other visual makers who use AI generation, reference images, and generated video outputs as part of a practical creative workflow. They need fast, browser-based tools that help them move from prompt or media output to usable assets without switching products.

## Product Purpose

The product provides a ComfyUI-backed AI image and video creation workspace with chat-style prompting, workflow selection, media generation, session history, and asset utilities. Success means users can generate, inspect, refine, extract, and export creative assets with clear controls and predictable results.

## Brand Personality

Clear, professional, creator-friendly. The interface should feel capable and calm rather than flashy, with enough guidance for non-technical creators and enough control for experienced users.

## Anti-references

Avoid toy-like one-click converters, cramped parameter dumps, unclear export states, and generic enterprise dashboards that hide the creative workflow behind heavy chrome. Avoid making the video-to-frame tools feel like an unrelated utility bolted onto the app.

## Design Principles

Keep the creative output central.
Expose professional controls progressively.
Make every processing step visible and reversible where possible.
Prefer clear production language over decorative copy.
Preserve the existing ai-draw product shell and interaction model.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve keyboard access for frame selection and export controls. Support reduced-motion preferences and keep the workflow usable on mobile and tablet screens.
